## How to annotate PDF files online

- Open Helperbird and select the PDF reader option.
- Click the open PDF button.
- Click annotate button along the top.
- Thats it.
      





## How to highlight PDF files online

- Open Helperbird and select the PDF reader option.
- Click the open PDF button.
- Click annotate button along the top.
- Thats it.
