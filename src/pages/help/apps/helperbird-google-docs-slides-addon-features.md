The Helperbird add-on for Google Docs and Slides brings powerful accessibility features directly into your documents and presentations. Here's what you can do.

---

## Translation

### Translate Selected Text

Highlight any text in your document or slide and translate it into over 100 languages. Choose to view the translation or replace the original text with the translated version.

### Translate & Replace

Perfect for working with multilingual documents. Translate text and replace it in one click, maintaining your document's formatting.

---

## Text to Speech

### Listen to Your Documents

Have your documents and slides read aloud to you. Select text and click play to hear it spoken in a natural voice.

### Multiple Voices

Choose from different voice options and adjust the reading speed to match your preference.

---

## Dyslexia Support

### Dyslexia-Friendly Fonts

Apply fonts designed for easier reading, like OpenDyslexic, directly to your selected text.

### Text Formatting

Adjust letter spacing, word spacing, and line height to make text more readable.

---

## Additional Features

### Summarize Text

Get quick summaries of selected text to understand key points faster.

### Word Count & Statistics

View detailed statistics about your selected text including word count, character count, and reading time.

### Immersive Reader

Open selected text in Microsoft's Immersive Reader for an enhanced reading experience with additional customization options.

---

## How to Use Features

### Step 1: Open Helperbird

In Google Docs or Slides, click **Extensions** in the top menu, then select **Helperbird** to open the sidebar.

---

### Step 2: Select Your Text

Highlight the text you want to work with in your document or slide.

---

### Step 3: Choose a Feature

Click on any feature in the Helperbird sidebar to apply it to your selected text.

**Tip:** Most features work instantly. For translation, you'll be prompted to choose a target language before the translation appears.

---

## Pro Features

Some features require Helperbird Pro. If you see a lock icon next to a feature, upgrade to Pro to unlock it. Your Pro subscription works across the browser extension, Google add-on, and all Helperbird apps.
