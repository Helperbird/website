## How to Open the Helperbird Editor

The Helperbird Editor lets you easily edit and work with text on the web. Here’s how to open the
Helperbird Editor:

### 1. Open Helperbird

Click the Helperbird icon in your browser’s extension area, usually in the top right corner of your
browser window.

### 2. Find the Editor

The Helperbird interface will open. Look for the "Open Editor" icon, which is marked with a pencil
symbol.

### 3. Open the Editor

Click on the **Open Editor** icon to start editing text.

## How to Create a New Document in the Helperbird Editor

Creating a new document is simple. Here’s how:

### 1. Open the Editor

Click on the Helperbird icon to open the Editor if it’s not already open.

### 2. Start a New Document

At the top of the Editor, find the **New** button, which might look like a blank page icon. Click it
to open a fresh, blank document.

### 3. Begin Writing

Click into the blank document and start typing your content.

## How to Open Existing Documents in the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Open the Editor

Open the Helperbird Editor app.

### 2. Find Your Document

Click on the **Open Document** icon, usually represented by a folder or document symbol.

### 3. Select Your Document

Browse the list of your saved documents and select the one you want to work on.

### 4. Start Editing

The document will open in the Editor, ready for you to edit or review.

## How to Save Your Document in the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Finish Your Work

Complete your content in the Editor.

### 2. Save Your Document

Click the **Save** button at the top of the Editor window to store your document securely.

## How to Copy a Document in the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Open Your Document

Make sure all your latest changes are in place.

### 2. Copy the Document

Click the **Copy Document** button, usually at the top of the Editor window, to create a duplicate.

### 3. Find the Copied Document

The copied document will appear in your documents area.

---

---

## How to Delete a Document in the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Open the Document

Ensure the document you want to delete is open in the Editor.

### 2. Delete the Document

Click the **Delete** button at the top of the Editor window. Confirm the deletion if prompted.

### 3. Confirm Deletion

The document will be permanently removed from your collection.

## How to Access Text Analytics in the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Open Text Analytics

With your document open, locate the "Text Analytics" button on the top toolbar of the Editor.

### 2. View Document Statistics

Click the **Text Analytics** button to see a detailed breakdown of your document's statistics,
including word count, character count, and readability scores.

### 3. Use the Data

Use these insights to improve your writing or get a snapshot of your style.

## How to Import Notes into the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Open the Editor

Open your document in the Helperbird Editor.

### 2. Import Notes

Find the **Import Notes** button on the top toolbar. Click it to open the notes sidebar.

### 3. Add Your Notes

Drag your notes into the sidebar. The Editor will create references, organizing your content and
notes side by side.

---

---

## How to Export Your Notes from the Helperbird Editor

### 1. Finalize Your Notes

Complete any final edits in the Editor.

### 2. Export Options

Click the **Export** button to reveal export options.

#### Export to Word

Choose **Download to Word** to save your notes as a .docx file.

#### Export to Markdown

Select **Download to Markdown** to save your notes as a .md file.

#### Export to Google Docs

Choose **Download to Google Docs** to create a new Google Docs document in your Drive.

#### Export to Text File

Select **Download to Text file** to save your notes as a .txt file.

Now your notes are ready to be shared or used on different platforms!
