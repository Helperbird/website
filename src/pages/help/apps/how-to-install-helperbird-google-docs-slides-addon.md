The Helperbird add-on brings accessibility features directly into Google Docs and Google Slides. Here's how to install it.

---

## Install from Google Docs

### Step 1: Open a Google Doc

Go to [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com) and open any document, or create a new one.

---

### Step 2: Open the Extensions Menu

Click **Extensions** in the top menu bar.

---

### Step 3: Get Add-ons

Select **Add-ons** from the dropdown, then click **Get add-ons**. This opens the Google Workspace Marketplace.

---

### Step 4: Search for Helperbird

In the search bar, type **Helperbird** and press Enter.

---

### Step 5: Install

Click on the Helperbird add-on in the results, then click the **Install** button. Follow the prompts to grant the necessary permissions.

**Note:** If you're using a work or school account, you may need admin approval to install add-ons.

---

## Install from Google Slides

### Step 1: Open a Google Slides Presentation

Go to [Google Slides](https://slides.google.com) and open any presentation, or create a new one.

---

### Step 2: Open the Extensions Menu

Click **Extensions** in the top menu bar.

---

### Step 3: Get Add-ons

Select **Add-ons** from the dropdown, then click **Get add-ons**. This opens the Google Workspace Marketplace.

---

### Step 4: Search and Install

Search for **Helperbird**, click on it, and then click **Install**. Follow the prompts to complete the installation.

---

## Install from Google Workspace Marketplace

You can also install directly from the marketplace:

### Step 1: Visit the Marketplace

Go to the [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace) directly.

---

### Step 2: Search for Helperbird

Type **Helperbird** in the search bar and press Enter.

---

### Step 3: Install

Click on the Helperbird add-on and then click **Install**. You'll be prompted to sign in to your Google account and grant permissions.

---

## After Installation

Once installed, you can access Helperbird from the **Extensions** menu in any Google Doc or Slides presentation. Click **Extensions > Helperbird** to open the sidebar and start using accessibility features.

**Tip:** The add-on works alongside the Helperbird browser extension. Having both installed gives you the best experience across Google products.
