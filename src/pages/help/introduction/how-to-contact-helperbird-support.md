Need help with Helperbird? Our support team is here to assist you. Here's how to reach us.

---

## Email Support

The fastest way to get help is to email us directly.

**Email:** support@helperbird.com

Send us a message and we'll get back to you as quickly as possible, usually within 24-48 hours.

---

## Support Form

### Step 1: Visit the Support Page

Go to the [Helperbird support page](https://www.helperbird.com/support/).

---

### Step 2: Fill Out the Form

Enter your name, email address, and describe your issue or question in the message field.

---

### Step 3: Submit

Click **Send** and we'll respond to your email.

---

## Tips for Faster Help

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

**Browser and Version**
Let us know which browser you're using (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) and the version number.

**Description of the Issue**
Tell us what's happening, what you expected to happen, and any error messages you see.

**Steps to Reproduce**
If you can, list the steps that lead to the problem so we can recreate it.

**Screenshots**
A screenshot can help us understand the issue faster.

**Subscription Status**
Let us know if you're using Helperbird Free or Pro so we can check your account.
