## View Your Subscription

### Step 1: Go to the Subscription Portal

Visit the [Helperbird subscription portal](https://www.helperbird.com/subscription/) to view your current subscription status.

---

### Step 2: Log In

Enter the email address you used when you signed up for Helperbird Pro. You'll receive a login link to access your account.

---

### Step 3: Check Your Status

Once logged in, you can see your subscription status, next billing date, and subscription details.

---

## Update Payment Method

### Step 1: Access Your Account

Go to the [Helperbird subscription portal](https://www.helperbird.com/subscription/) and log in with your email.

---

### Step 2: Click Update Payment

In your account dashboard, look for the **Update Payment Method** option.

---

### Step 3: Enter New Details

Add your new payment card details and save. Your next payment will use the new method.

---

## Cancel Your Subscription

### Step 1: Access Your Account

Go to the [Helperbird subscription portal](https://www.helperbird.com/subscription/) and log in with your email.

---

### Step 2: Click Cancel Subscription

In your account dashboard, click **Cancel Subscription**.

---

### Step 3: Confirm Cancellation

Follow the prompts to confirm. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

**Note:** After cancellation, you'll still have access to all Helperbird Free features.
