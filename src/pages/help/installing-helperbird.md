## How to install Helperbird

### How to install Helperbird for Google Chrome

**Step 1:** Open Google Chrome on your computer.

**Step 2:** Go to the Helperbird extension page on the Chrome Web Store by searching for "Helperbird" or by visiting this link: [https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/helperbird/ahmapmilbkfamljbpgphfndeemhnajme](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/helperbird/ahmapmilbkfamljbpgphfndeemhnajme)

**Step 3:** Click on the "Add to Chrome" button on the Helperbird extension page.

**Step 4:** A pop-up window will appear asking you to confirm the installation. 

**Step 5:** Click on "Add extension" to proceed.

**Step 6:** The Helperbird extension will now be installed on your Chrome browser, and you will see a Helperbird icon in the top-right corner of your browser.

**Optional:** You can pin the Helperbird icon to the top-right corner of your browser by clicking on the three dots next to the Helperbird icon, and then clicking on "Pin to toolbar".







### How to install Helperbird for Microsoft Edge

**Step 1:** Open Microsoft Edge on your computer.

**Step 2:** Go to the Microsoft Edge Add-ons store by visiting this link: [https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/)

**Step 3:** Search for "Helperbird" or directly visit this link: (https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/helperbird/gfjkfjnhkpncjfjgcfhfhljomfejnpbk)[https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/helperbird/gfjkfjnhkpncjfjgcfhfhljomfejnpbk]

**Step 4:** Click on the "Get" button to install the Helperbird extension.

**Step 5:** A pop-up window will appear asking you to confirm the installation. Click on "Add extension" to proceed.

**Step 6:** The Helperbird extension will now be installed on your Microsoft Edge browser, and you will see a Helperbird icon in the top-right corner of your browser.










### How to install Helperbird for Mozilla Firefox



**Step 1:** Open Firefox on your computer.

**Step 2:** Go to the Firefox Add-ons store by visiting this link: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/)

**Step 3:** Search for "Helperbird" or directly visit this link: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/helperbird/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/)

**Step 4:**  Click on the "Add to Firefox" button to install the Helperbird extension.

**Step 5:**  A pop-up window will appear asking you to confirm the installation. Click on "Add" to proceed.

**Step 6:** The Helperbird extension will now be installed on your Firefox browser, and you will see a Helperbird icon in the top-right corner of your browser.





## How to add the Helperbird add-on to Google Docs

**Step 1:** Open a Google Docs document.

**Step 2:** Click on the "Add-ons" menu in the top toolbar.

Step 3:** Select "Get add-ons" from the drop-down menu.

**Step 4:** Search for "Helperbird" in the G Suite Marketplace.

**Step 5:** Click on the "Helperbird" add-on listing.

**Step 6:** Click on the "Install" button to install the add-on.

**Step 7:** A pop-up window will appear asking for permission to install the add-on. Click on "Allow" to continue.

**Step 8:** The Helperbird add-on will now be installed and available to use in the "Add-ons" menu.

**Step 9:** To use the add-on, select "Helperbird" from the "Add-ons" menu, and follow the prompts to customize your settings and start using the features.

**Step 10:** (Optional) To manage or remove the add-on, go to the "Add-ons" menu and select "Manage add-ons". You will be able to see the add-ons that are installed and have the option to remove them.

### Please note
It may require additional steps such as signing in to your account or admin approval.

## How to download Helperbird app on iPad

**Step 1:** Open the App Store on your iPad.

**Step 2:** Search for [Helperbird](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/helperbird-for-safari/id1589138053 'Helperbird for Safari link') in the search bar at the top of the screen.

**Step 3:** Locate the Helperbird app from the search results.

**Step 4:** Tap on the "GET" or "INSTALL" button to start the download and installation process.

**Step 5:** Once the installation is completed, the app icon will appear on your home screen.

**Step 6:** Tap on the Helperbird app icon to open the app.

**Step 7:** Follow the on-screen instructions to sign in or create an account.

**Step 8:** Once you are logged in, you can start using the app's features.

### Please note 
The Helperbird app might require additional steps such as signing in to your account or connecting to another service. Also, The app might not be available in all regions or languages.

Also note that you need to have an iPad running at least iOS **15.0** or later to install and run the Helperbird app.
